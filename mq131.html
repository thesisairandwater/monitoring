MQ131 Sensor

Description

The MQ131 is a highly sensitive ozone (O3) gas sensor that detects ozone concentrations in the air. It operates by measuring changes in resistance across its internal sensing element. It is widely used in air quality monitoring systems and industrial applications for ozone leak detection and environmental safety.

With its robust design and reliable performance, the MQ131 sensor is a favorite for projects requiring ozone level measurement in both indoor and outdoor environments.

What is Ozone (O3)?

Ozone (O3): A molecule composed of three oxygen atoms, ozone exists naturally in the Earth's atmosphere. While it is essential in the stratosphere for blocking harmful UV radiation, ground-level ozone can be a pollutant. It forms due to reactions between sunlight, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and nitrogen oxides (NOx). High concentrations of ground-level ozone can cause respiratory problems, harm vegetation, and damage materials.

Fun Facts

  • Sensitivity: Detects ozone concentrations as low as 10 ppb (parts per billion).
  • Applications: Commonly used in air purifiers, ozone generators, and environmental safety systems.
  • Durability: Long lifespan when used within recommended conditions.
  • Versatility: Effective in both industrial and home settings.
  • Calibration: Can be calibrated for accurate measurements in specific environments.

Additional Information

  • Power Requirements: Operates at 5V DC with minimal power consumption.
  • Output: Provides analog and digital signals for easy integration with microcontrollers.
  • Temperature Range: Functions effectively in temperatures between -20°C and 50°C.
  • Compact Design: Measuring only 35mm x 20mm x 16mm, it’s ideal for embedded systems.
  • Maintenance: Periodic calibration ensures accuracy and reliability.
